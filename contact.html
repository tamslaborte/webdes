Visit Pirlem Museum

Pirlem Museum is the first (and to date, the only) museum in the Philippines that has set its mission around Filipino children and culture. Pirlem Museum's mission is to strengthen the knowledge, values and cultural identity of Romblon Province.

With this mission in mind, the museum has launched exhibits and programs anchored on the Romblon Province culture, heritage and history. Pirlem Museum is currently guided by its four pillars: care for the environment; health; kindness and compassion.
