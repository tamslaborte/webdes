Thank You Pirlem Volunteers!

If not for our dedicated and passionate volunteers, Pirlem Museum would not be as successful as we are today. Your commitment compell us towards our shared goal of promoting and preserving Pirlem history, heritage and culture. Join us as we work together towards achieving this goal. Be a volunteer today!
